Audit Readiness

Automated ISO & ASME IX Compliance

Elenchon translates your factory-floor operations into immutable, version-pinned compliance ledgers. Every qualification, variable, and process is verified and locked the moment it is captured. When the auditors arrive, your data is instantaneous, undisputed, and fully prepared.

Powered by Phrakton

Military-Grade Protection for Your IP

Elenchon’s uncompromising local performance is protected by Phrakton, our zero-trust network overlay built on OpenZiti. Phrakton abstracts the network layer entirely: your factory infrastructure becomes invisible to external threat actors while remaining seamlessly accessible to authorized personnel — with full multitenant isolation between facilities and post-quantum-safe encryption end to end.

  • The Invisible Factory: Services are unreachable from the public internet — no open ports, no attack surface, no lateral network movement, no corporate espionage.
  • Secure Remote Oversight: Monitor operations and conduct audits from home or abroad through a post-quantum-safe encrypted tunnel.
  • Multitenant by Design: Each facility operates in its own cryptographically isolated tenant — strict separation, centrally managed.
  • Absolute Data Sovereignty: Your manufacturing data never touches a shared cloud server. The physical hardware remains entirely under your jurisdiction.
