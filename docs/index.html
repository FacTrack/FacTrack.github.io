Seamless Engineering Integration

Eliminate manual data entry and transcription errors. WeldTrack features a direct CAD/BOM ingestion pipeline, automatically mapping columns from engineering software (like Tekla) to perfectly align your 3D models with shop-floor execution.

10-Second Floor Execution

Your welders should be welding, not swiping on tablets. Our mobile-first interface and instant QR code scanning allow operators to pinpoint precise project nodes in under 10 seconds, virtually eliminating software dwell time.

Offline-Resilient Documentation

Critical blueprints, WPS documents, and build plans are instantly accessible to floor workers via one-click caching. Production and quality checks continue uninterrupted, even in total Wi-Fi dead zones within the factory.

Asset & Maintenance Lifecycle

Centralize your machine readiness. WeldTrack includes a dedicated equipment hub with prioritized, 4-tier ticketing for maintenance requests, ensuring all factory hardware and welder certifications remain continuously valid.

Automated ISO & ASME IX Compliance

WeldTrack translates your factory-floor operations into immutable, version-pinned compliance ledgers. Every qualification, variable, and process is mathematically verified. When the auditors arrive, your data is instantaneous, undisputed, and fully prepared.
