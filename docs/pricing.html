Common Questions

Pricing, Answered Before You Ask

The questions we get on every first call — answered here so you don’t have to make the call to get them.

How much does Elenchon cost?

Lite is €400 per month with 20 workers included. Standard is €880 per month with 50 workers included. Both are flat rates per appliance, per month, excluding VAT — no per-seat traps and no usage surcharges.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, and it costs nothing. Upgrades, downgrades and add-ons are applied remotely over the Phrakton fabric — no engineer visit, no commute fee. Upgrades apply immediately; downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.

How large a floor does Lite cover?

Lite includes 20 workers and takes one expansion block of ten — a 30-worker ceiling. Past that you move to Standard, which includes 50 and expands without limit. Moving up is a remote plan change, not a re-installation.

What comes with a worker block?

€90 per month adds ten workers, and every block carries 20 GB of recurring remote bandwidth at no extra charge. If you need more than that, bandwidth is €25 per 100 GB.

What does installation cost?

Standard is €1,700 all in — €850 deployment and €850 floor training, quoted as one line. Lite ships as a single €1,100 package covering both. Hardware is passed through at cost — roughly €300–€400 — with zero markup.

What if the fabric goes down?

Production doesn’t stop — Elenchon runs locally on your appliance, so an outage only affects access from outside the facility. And we pay for it: 10% off the invoice below 99.9% uptime, 50% below 99%, 75% below 95%, and below 90% the entire month is free. Credits apply automatically.

What happens if we cancel?

There is no lock-in and no exit fee. The appliance is yours and the data never left it, so you keep permanent view-only access to your historical records for audits.
