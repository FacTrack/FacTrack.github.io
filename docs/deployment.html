How It Works

From First Call to Live Floor

A structured, low-friction path — your production never stops while we work.

1

Scoping Session

A focused call with our founders to map your project structures, compliance requirements, and floor workflows — and confirm WeldTrack fits your operation.

2

On-Site Installation

Our team delivers and configures the hardware-secured server appliance directly at your facility. A formal agreement guarantees absolute legal and industrial data confidentiality.

3

Onboarding & Go-Live

We import your first projects, configure roles and templates with your managers, and train floor personnel — production continues uninterrupted throughout.

Zero Lock-In Risk

Your data lives on your hardware from day one. Cancel at any time and retain permanent view-only access to all historical records for audits — no hostage data, ever.

Limited Capacity, By Design

We don’t ship boxes and wish you luck. Every deployment gets our engineers on your floor, which caps how many we take per quarter. When the quarter’s slots are gone, they’re gone.
